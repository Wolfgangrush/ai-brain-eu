## What to look for

- **Legal terminology**: Each jurisdiction uses specific terms (e.g., "Maître" vs "Rechtsanwalt" vs "avvocato")
- **Bar-specific language**: Some countries use formal/informal registers differently
- **Natural phrasing**: AI translations can be grammatically correct but sound unnatural
- **Cultural appropriateness**: The tone should match what a lawyer in that jurisdiction expects
- **Arabic RTL**: Verify right-to-left text renders correctly in markdown and terminals
