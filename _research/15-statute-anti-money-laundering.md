## Authority Tier
Statute (Regulation (EU) 2024/1624)

## Currency Warning
The 2024 AML Package is the new "Gold Standard." The AML Regulation becomes directly applicable on **July 10, 2027**. The new authority (AMLA) begins operations in **2025**.

## Key Facts
1. **Single Rulebook:** For the first time, AML rules for lawyers are harmonized in a directly applicable Regulation, removing national variances.
2. **Obliged Entities:** Lawyers are "obliged entities" when assisting in high-risk transactions: real estate, managing assets, creating companies/trusts, or managing bank accounts.
3. **Three Lines of Defense:** Even small firms must have: 1) Staff-level CDD, 2) A management-level Compliance Officer, and 3) An independent audit (proportionate to size).
4. **Customer Due Diligence (CDD):** Must identify and verify the client and the Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO). Discrepancies between the lawyer's info and the Central Register must be reported.
5. **Legal Professional Privilege (LPP):** Remains protected. Lawyers are generally exempt from reporting if the info was obtained while "ascertaining the legal position" or defending a client in court.
6. **Cash Limit:** A new EU-wide limit of **10,000 EUR** for cash payments (some states have lower limits, e.g., 1,000 EUR in France/Italy).
7. **AMLA Supervision:** The new EU AML Authority (AMLA) will oversee national bar associations to ensure they are strictly supervising law firms' compliance.

## Verbatim Quote (if applicable)
"Lawyers ... should not be required to report information ... which they receive from, or obtain on, one of their clients, in the course of ascertaining the legal position of that client." - Recital, Regulation 2024/1624.

## Practical Implication for Solo Advocate
- **KYC Software:** Solo advocates must implement automated KYC/UBO check tools to comply with the documentation burden of the 2024 Regulation.
- **Client Onboarding:** "Red flag" any client insisting on cash payments above 10k EUR or refusing to identify the UBO.
- **Internal Manual:** By 2027, the advocate must have a written "AML Policy" even if they practice alone.
