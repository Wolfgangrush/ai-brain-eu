## Sources tried but rejected (and why)
- Random legal blog templates: rejected as non-primary sources.
- National DPA templates (except as triangulation): avoided where a supranational EDPB or Commission template was available to ensure EU-wide relevance.
- LegalZoom / RocketLawyer-style consumer-template sites: rejected as tertiary.

## Currency warnings (current)

- **TTBER 316/2014** expires 30 April 2026 — track replacement regulation.
- **Data Act 2023/2854** — main provisions apply from 12 September 2025; switching-charge elimination 12 January 2027.
- **MiCA 2023/1114** — CASP transitional period expires 1 July 2026 (Member State option to shorten).
- **DORA 2022/2554** — applies from 17 January 2025; financial entities must update ICT contracts.
- **ECHR Protocol No 15** — 4-month application time-limit (not 6 months) since 1 August 2022.
- **ODR Platform Regulation 524/2013** repealed effective 20 July 2025; certified national ADR bodies remain available.
- **Brussels Ia Recast** — exequatur abolished; refusal grounds in Arts 45-46.
- **EU Insolvency Recast 2015/848** — applies to proceedings opened after 26 June 2017.
- **AI Act** — Tier-1 obligations (Technical Documentation · FRIA · Transparency) deadline 2 August 2026.
- **NIS2** — 24-hour Early Warning is a strict compliance trigger.
- **CJEU appeal filter** — Article 58a Statute requires prior allowance for appeal-board appeals (in force since 1 May 2019).

## Known gaps (intentional · deferred to subsequent waves)

- **Member State pleading templates** — handled in respective Member State corpora (DE · FR · IT · ES · IE · NL · PL · etc.); EU corpus focuses on supranational forms.
- **ECB Administrative Board of Review** and **EPO Boards of Appeal** — Tier-3 specialist tribunals reserved for later expansion.
- **Sector-specific Data Spaces** (Health Data Space · Mobility Data Space · Financial Data Space) — pending Regulation finalisation.
- **AI Act technical Annexes** for high-risk AI categories — pending CEN / CENELEC standards (late 2026).
- **Anti-suit and anti-arbitration injunctions** — interaction with Brussels Ia limited by *West Tankers* and *Gazprom* jurisprudence; specialist note reserved.

## Recommended next step

- Indemnity vs compensation choice under Art 17 Commercial Agents Directive per Member State.
