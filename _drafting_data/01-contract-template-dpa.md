# GDPR Article 28 — Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

## Source URL
[Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/915](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32021D0915)

## Last Verified
2026-05-20

## Authority Tier
Primary - European Commission Implementing Decision under GDPR Art 28(7).

## Currency Warning
Current as of 2026. These clauses provide a "safe harbor" for meeting Article 28 requirements within the EU/EEA. If data is transferred outside the EEA, these must be supplemented by the Transfer SCCs (2021/914).

## Template Type
Contract / Regulatory Template

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Section I: General Provisions**
   - Clause 1: Purpose and scope (meeting Art 28(3) and (4) requirements)
   - Clause 2: Invariability of the Clauses (standard text must remain unchanged)
   - Clause 3: Interpretation
   - Clause 4: Hierarchy (prevails over conflicting terms in MSA)
   - Clause 5: Docking clause (Optional - allows new parties to join)
2. **Section II: Obligations of the Parties**
   - Clause 6: Description of processing(s) (refers to Annex II)
   - Clause 7: Obligations of the Parties
     - 7.1 Instructions (documented instructions from controller)
     - 7.2 Purpose limitation
     - 7.3 Duration of processing
     - 7.4 Security of processing (requires Annex III)
     - 7.5 Sensitive data (special safeguards)
     - 7.6 Documentation and compliance (audits)
     - 7.7 Use of sub-processors (Specific or General authorization)
     - 7.8 Onward transfers (Chapter V compliance)
   - Clause 8: Assistance to the controller (Data subject rights, DPIAs)
   - Clause 9: Notification of personal data breach (to the controller)
3. **Section III: Final Provisions**
   - Clause 10: Non-compliance and termination
4. **Annexes**
   - Annex I: List of parties
   - Annex II: Description of the processing (Categories of data subjects, types of data, etc.)
   - Annex III: Technical and organisational measures (TOMs)
   - Annex IV: List of sub-processors (if specific authorization used)

## Drafting Conventions
- **Instruction Principle**: The processor is strictly bound by the controller's documented instructions.
- **Sub-processing Authorization**: Parties must choose between **Option 1: Prior Specific Authorization** (naming every sub-processor) or **Option 2: General Written Authorization** (with a notice period for changes).
- **Assistance Duty**: The processor must provide "reasonable assistance" to the controller for fulfilling data subject requests and security obligations.
- **Audit Rights**: The processor must make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow for audits/inspections.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The processor shall process personal data only on documented instructions from the controller, unless required to do so by Union or Member State law... The processor shall immediately inform the controller if, in its opinion, an instruction infringes the GDPR or other Union or Member State data protection provisions." (Clause 7.1).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The standard template used for all service provider contracts (SaaS, cloud hosting, outsourced paralegal services) where the provider handles personal data on behalf of the firm or its clients. Using the Commission's standard text reduces the need for heavy negotiation with vendors.
