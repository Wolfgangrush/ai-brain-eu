# DMA — Designation Challenge / Rebuttal Template (Form GD)

## Source URL
- [EU Digital Markets Act (Regulation (EU) 2022/1925) — Article 3](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32022R1925#d1e670-1-1)
- [Commission Implementing Regulation (EU) 2023/814 — Annex I (Form GD)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32023R0814)

## Last Verified
2026-05-20

## Authority Tier
Primary - Statutory requirement and Implementing Regulation for the DMA.

## Currency Warning
Current as of 2026. The 30-page limit for rebuttals is strictly enforced by the Commission's DG COMP.

## Template Type
Pleading / Regulatory Submission

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Section 1 & 2: Undertaking Identification**
   - Details of the undertaking and authorized representatives.
2. **Section 3 & 4: Quantitative Threshold Data**
   - Annual EU turnover / Average Market Capitalization.
   - Monthly active end users (45M+ threshold).
   - Yearly active business users (10,000+ threshold).
   - Identification of "Core Platform Services" (CPS) meeting the thresholds.
3. **Section 5: Rebuttal of the Gatekeeper Presumption (The "Challenge")**
   - **Exceptional Circumstances**: Evidence demonstrating that despite meeting the thresholds, the undertaking does not satisfy the qualitative criteria:
     - Lack of a significant impact on the internal market.
     - Not an important gateway for business users to reach end users.
     - Does not enjoy an entrenched and durable position.
   - **Substantiated Arguments**: Detailed analysis for each challenged CPS (provided in separate annexes).
4. **Section 6: Confidentiality and Waiver**
   - Identification of business secrets.
   - Signed declaration of completeness and accuracy.

## Drafting Conventions
- **30-Page Limit**: Rebuttals must not exceed 30 pages per CPS.
- **Separate Annexes**: Each core platform service must have its own dedicated rebuttal annex.
- **Completeness**: The 45-working-day clock for the Commission's decision only starts when the filing is deemed "complete."
- **Language**: Any of the 24 official EU languages.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The undertaking... may provide, with its notification, sufficiently substantiated arguments to demonstrate that, in the circumstances in which the relevant core platform service operates, and taking into account the elements listed in paragraph 8, the undertaking exceptionally does not satisfy the requirements listed in paragraph 1." (DMA Art 3(5)).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Used by specialist counsel representing tech companies that meet the quantitative thresholds of the DMA but operate in niche or competitive markets where they lack "gatekeeper" power. The template ensures all mandatory fields of Form GD are populated to avoid procedural rejection.
