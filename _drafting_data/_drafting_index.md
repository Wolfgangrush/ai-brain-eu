## Scope Coverage (v0.2 corpus)
- Data protection · AI Act · digital services · cybersecurity compliance: complete (10 templates).
- CJEU / General Court litigation: pleadings · motions · interim relief · appeals · intervention · evidence (10 templates).
- Cross-border enforcement: EAPO · EEO · EOP · Small Claims · Brussels Ia recognition (5 instruments).
- ADR: Mediation Directive · settlement enforceability · consumer ADR (3 templates).
- EU insolvency: main + secondary proceedings under Recast Regulation (2 templates).
- EU tribunals: EUIPO Boards of Appeal (1 template; ECB ABoR + EPO Boards reserved for later wave).
- EU commercial: Commercial Agents · VBER (franchise + distribution) · TTBER · Data Act SaaS · DGA · Public Procurement · MiCA · DORA (9 templates).

## Deferred to v0.3 / v0.4 (intentional)
- ECB Administrative Board of Review · EPO Boards of Appeal · Member State pleading templates (national corpora).
- Sector-specific Data Spaces (Health · Mobility · Financial) — pending Regulation finalisation.
- AI Act technical Annex templates for high-risk AI categories — pending CEN/CENELEC standards.
