# ECHR — Application Form Template (Rule 47)

## Source URL
- [Rules of Court — Rule 47](https://www.echr.coe.int/documents/d/echr/rules_court)
- [Official ECHR Application Form](https://www.echr.coe.int/how-to-apply)

## Last Verified
2026-05-20

## Authority Tier
Primary - Rules of Court and official application form of the ECHR.

## Currency Warning
Current as of 2026. The time limit for lodging an application is **4 months** from the date of the final domestic decision. Scanned or digital signatures are generally rejected; original ink signatures are required.

## Template Type
Court Form / Human Rights

## Structural Skeleton
1. **The Applicant (Sections A.1 & A.2)**
   - Individuals: Name, DOB, nationality, address.
   - Organizations: Name, registration number, address.
2. **Respondent State(s) (Section B)**
   - Identification of the Contracting Party/Parties against which the application is made.
3. **Representation (Section C)**
   - Details of the lawyer or non-lawyer representative.
4. **Authority (Section D) - CRITICAL**
   - Mandatory original signatures of both applicant and representative.
5. **Statement of Facts (Section E)**
   - Concise, chronological summary of the events (must fit within the form's fields).
6. **Violations and Arguments (Section F)**
   - Specific Articles of the Convention/Protocols and supporting arguments.
7. **Domestic Remedies and 4-Month Rule (Section G)**
   - List of all domestic court decisions and dates to prove exhaustion and timeliness.
8. **Other Proceedings (Section H)**
   - Disclosure of any other international submissions.
9. **List of Documents (Section I)**
   - Numbered, chronological list of all supporting documents.
10. **Declaration and Signature (Section J)**
    - Final original signature by the applicant or representative.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Self-Sufficiency**: The form must stand alone. Do not simply say "see attached"; the core facts and violations must be summarized *within* the form's boxes.
- **Ink Signatures**: Section D (Authority) and Section J (Declaration) MUST contain original ink signatures.
- **Page Limits**: A separate supplement of no more than **20 pages** is permitted, but the form itself must be fully completed first.
- **Postal Submission**: Applications must be sent by post; e-mail or fax submissions are not valid for lodging a new case.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"An application... shall be made on the form provided by the Registry... It shall set out: (a) the name, date of birth, nationality and address of the applicant... (b) the name of the Contracting Party or Parties against which the application is made... (c) a concise and legible statement of the facts... (d) a concise and legible statement of the alleged violations of the Convention." (Rules of Court, Rule 47(1)).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
The final avenue for redress after exhausting all national court options. Solo advocates use this template to ensure the application passes the ECHR's strict "administrative filter." Over 90% of applications are rejected for formal non-compliance; following this template exactly is a professional necessity.
