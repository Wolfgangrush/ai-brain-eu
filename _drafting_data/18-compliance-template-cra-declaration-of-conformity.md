# EU Cyber Resilience Act — Product Declaration of Conformity Template

## Source URL
[EU Cyber Resilience Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/2847) — Annex V](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32024R2847)

## Last Verified
2026-05-20

## Authority Tier
Primary - Statutory requirement under the Cyber Resilience Act.

## Currency Warning
Current as of 2026. This Declaration must be issued before any "product with digital elements" is placed on the EU market. For "Critical Products" (Annex IV), third-party assessment by a Notified Body is mandatory.

## Template Type
Compliance / Technical Template

## Structural Skeleton
1. **Product Identification**
   - Unique identifier (Model, type, batch, or serial number).
2. **Manufacturer Details**
   - Name and address of the manufacturer (and authorized representative, if applicable).
3. **Sole Responsibility Statement**
   - Explicit declaration that the manufacturer is solely responsible for compliance.
4. **Object of the Declaration**
   - Description and traceability (including images if necessary for identification).
5. **Legislation Compliance Statement**
   - Declaration of conformity with Regulation (EU) 2024/2847 (Cyber Resilience Act).
6. **References to Standards**
   - List of harmonized standards (e.g., ETSI EN 303 645) or common specifications used.
7. **Notified Body Intervention (For Annex IV Critical Products)**
   - Name and number of the Notified Body.
   - Description of the procedure (e.g., EU-type examination).
   - Certificate number issued.
8. **Final Sign-off**
   - Place and date of issue.
   - Name, function, and signature of the authorized person.

## Drafting Conventions
- **Simplified Declaration (Annex VI)**: If the full declaration is not provided in print, a simplified version must be included with the product, containing a URL/QR code to the full document.
- **Language**: Must be translated into the language(s) required by the Member State where the product is made available.
- **Durable Medium**: The manufacturer must keep the declaration for 10 years after the product is placed on the market.
- **Alignment with NLF**: The structure follows the New Legislative Framework (NLF) standard for all EU product safety declarations.

## Fair-Use Excerpt (if applicable)
"The EU declaration of conformity shall state that the fulfilment of the essential requirements set out in Annex I has been demonstrated. The EU declaration of conformity shall have the model structure set out in Annex V and shall be updated. It shall be translated into the language or languages required by the Member State in which the product with digital elements is placed on the market." (CRA Art 20).

## Practical Use for Solo Advocate / Small Firm
Essential for clients manufacturing or importing IoT devices, software packages, or hardware components into the EU. Solo advocates use this to verify that a client's "Technical Documentation" supports the claims made in the Declaration of Conformity, particularly for critical products requiring Notified Body involvement.
